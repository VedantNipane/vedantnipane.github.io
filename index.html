About Me

Hi there! I'm Vedant, and I just wrapped up my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIIT Hyderabad. What really gets me excited is diving into data and finding stories hidden in the numbers. Recently, I spent a few months at Head Digital Works as an Associate Data Scientist, where I got to work with some impressive datasets – analyzing 5.6+ lakh gaming instances and 23+ crore in wagered amounts using Python and SQL. I also worked with fraud detection reports. It was fascinating to see how data could directly impact business decisions.

I'm one of those people who loves getting their hands dirty with different technologies. Whether it's building machine learning models, tinkering with IoT devices, or coding up web applications, I enjoy the challenge of making things work. I've had some great wins along the way too – like taking first place at the Hyderabad 5G-6G Hackathon and placing third nationally at India Mobile Congress with my IoT research. These experiences taught me that the best projects happen when you combine technical skills with real-world problem-solving

While I'm genuinely curious about various tech roles and love exploring new areas, my heart is really set on data science and machine learning opportunities. There's something incredibly satisfying about turning messy data into actionable insights that can actually make a difference. I'm drawn to roles where I can use data not just to answer questions, but to help businesses make smarter decisions and solve meaningful problems.
